Why AlphaTracker

Nowadays, startups could publish their cryptocurrencies to raise money. However, it brings high risks for ordinary investors, because it is difficult for people to distinguish a good company from a bad one, especially the company in the early days.

AlphaTracker is an investment info sharing and trading platform. Based on the fact that "Top angel investors could follow potential companies in early days" We filter and rank companies based on Twitter following relationship.

It helps ordinary people to invest in potential companies in early days to earn a good return. It also helps startup to raise money with its cryptocurrency.

How to use

View Companies

Click 'Companies' on the top, the default companies ranking is based on AlphaTracker's algorithm. Hover on the company name and you could see the details and links of the company.

You could click the column name to rank by yourself, search specific company, or filter by date.

'Collected Times' reflects how many people collect this company into their collections.

Fetch Companies

For new startups, if you'd like to add your company and cryptocurrency to our platform, simply register to us and type the company name in 'fetch', and click 'fetch', your company would be added and cryptocurrency could be showed and traded.

Collect/Uncollect

Collect the company you like to your personal collection.

You could set your collection visibility to 'Private/Public/Followers Only'. You could view others' collections based on their visibility setting.

Trade

Click trade of a company, you could see your total balance and shares held.

The cryptocurrency price chart of the company is real-time.

You could buy(go long) or sell(go short) of a cryptocurrency in our platform.

You could see the transaction history of this company in trading page, or see all transactions history in your profile.

Top Investors

Click 'Investors' on the top, there are top investors and their info.

The default order of investors is by their balance.

You could view their collections and collect their collected companies to your collection.

You could follow/unfollow these investors. Their updates will notify you in notification.

Communities

You could post Blog/News/Insights/General to the community, 'like/dislike' the post, or delete your post.

Live Chat

You could live chat anonymous with others.

Notifications

You could view your notifications by clicking the top notify icon.

Profile

Clicking your name on the top.

You could manage your collections, following investors, followers, and transaction history in your profile.
